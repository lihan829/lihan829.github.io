## About Me

I work at the intersection of **phonology, phonetics, and computation**, focusing on how tonal systems can be learned from data. My research emphasizes:

- Autosegmental representations (ARs)
- Tonotactic learning
- Interpretable computational models
- Learning under sparse data

I combine theoretical linguistics with algorithmic modeling to better understand how phonological structure is represented and learned.

---

## Research

### Current Focus
